The Conglomerate

About Arsh Group

Arsh Group of Companies is a multi-sector, dual-geography conglomerate that has grown from a single transport venture into a ₹1,500 Crore+ enterprise spanning twelve independent companies across India and the UAE. Headquartered in Bengaluru — India's commercial and technological capital — with operational presence in Dubai, the Group stands as the definitive symbol of entrepreneurial integrity in the subcontinent's business landscape.

Each entity within the Arsh ecosystem is architecturally distinct yet strategically interdependent. Real estate development feeds into hospitality investment; waste management operations generate ESG credentials that attract institutional capital; fragrance exports open luxury market access in the UAE; and consultancy services provide the intelligence layer guiding capital deployment across all verticals. This is not a collection of companies — it is an engineered ecosystem designed to grow with purpose and compound with scale.

In India, the Group has reshaped Bengaluru's residential landscape through Arsh Townships, pioneered eco-conscious urban infrastructure through Godfather Waste Management, and positioned Indian artisanal heritage on the global luxury stage through Arsh Royal Fragrances. In the UAE, it has established itself as a trusted partner in one of the world's most demanding commercial environments — earning its reputation through consistent delivery, not marketing.

The Group's competitive moat is not just diversification — it is the depth of founder-led operational intelligence, a network built over three decades, and a brand that HNI investors in both geographies recognise as synonymous with accountability and long-term value creation.
